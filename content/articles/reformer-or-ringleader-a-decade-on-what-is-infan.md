# Introduction
A decade has passed since Gianni Infantino took the reins of FIFA, succeeding the disgraced Sepp Blatter. The question on everyone's mind is: has Infantino been a reformer or a ringleader? To answer this, we must delve into his actions and decisions over the past ten years.

## Background
Infantino's rise to power was met with skepticism, given the corruption scandals that plagued FIFA under Blatter's leadership. However, Infantino promised to bring about change and transparency to the organization. But has he delivered on his promises?

### Expansion of the World Cup
One of Infantino's most notable decisions was the expansion of the World Cup from 32 teams to 48. While this move was seen as a way to increase participation and revenue, it has also been criticized for potentially diluting the quality of the tournament.

#### The 2022 World Cup in Qatar
The decision to award the 2022 World Cup to Qatar has been a contentious issue. Despite concerns over human rights and corruption, Infantino has maintained that the tournament will be a success. However, the controversy surrounding the bid process and the treatment of migrant workers has tarnished FIFA's reputation.

## Relationships with Powerful Figures
Infantino's relationships with powerful figures in the soccer world have also raised eyebrows. His close ties to Saudi Arabia's Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman have been particularly scrutinized. The Saudi prince's influence on FIFA has been significant, with the country hosting several high-profile tournaments and investing heavily in soccer infrastructure.

### The Platini Connection
Infantino's relationship with former UEFA president Michel Platini has also been a subject of interest. Platini, who was banned from soccer for eight years due to a corruption scandal, has been a vocal supporter of Infantino. This has led to accusations that Infantino is not truly committed to reforming FIFA.

## Conclusion
So, is Gianni Infantino a reformer or a ringleader? The answer is not straightforward. While he has made some positive changes, such as increasing transparency and expanding the World Cup, his relationships with powerful figures and his handling of controversial issues have raised concerns. As the soccer world looks to the future, it remains to be seen whether Infantino will continue to lead FIFA down a path of reform or succumb to the pressures of those who seek to exploit the organization for their own gain.

### Final Thoughts
The next decade will be crucial for FIFA and its president. With the 2026 World Cup on the horizon, Infantino must navigate the complex web of interests and alliances that shape the soccer world. Will he emerge as a true reformer, or will he be remembered as a ringleader who prioritized power and profit over the beautiful game?
