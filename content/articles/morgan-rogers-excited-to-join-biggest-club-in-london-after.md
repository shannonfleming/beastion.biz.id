The deal, which was agreed upon on Saturday, sees Rogers sign a seven-year contract with the Blues. This move has not only broken Chelsea's transfer record but has also stolen the spotlight from Arsenal, who were initially favorites to secure Rogers' signature.

Xabi Alonso, a key figure at Chelsea, played a significant role in convincing Rogers to join the club. The Spanish manager's influence and the club's ambition were instrumental in persuading the young English talent to choose Chelsea over other potential suitors.
