# Introduction
The National Women's Soccer League (NWSL) witnessed a historic moment on Saturday as the Denver Summit, the league's 16th franchise, shattered the single-game attendance record in their first home game. The match against Washington Spirit at Empower Field at Mile High Stadium drew an impressive crowd of 63,004 fans, surpassing the previous record by approximately 23,000 fans.

## The Rise of Denver Summit
Denver's entry into the NWSL has been nothing short of remarkable. Officially announced as the league's 16th team in January 2025, the franchise has been making waves with its rapid growth and ambitious approach. Within three days of their announcement, Denver Summit became the fastest expansion franchise to sell 5,000 season tickets, with 5,280 sold in that short period. The team went on to sell out their 8,500 season-ticket allotment quickly, adding thousands to a waitlist.

The record-breaking attendance is a testament to the growing popularity of women's soccer in the United States.
