# Introduction
Jeremy Doku, a name that has been making waves in the football world, has been working tirelessly to improve his game. Last month, he expressed his desire to add more goals to his repertoire, acknowledging that this was necessary to be considered among the best wingers globally. The question on everyone's mind now is: has he done enough to be part of that elite conversation?

## The Journey to Excellence
Doku's journey to becoming one of the most talked-about wingers in football has not been overnight. With dedication, hard work, and a passion for the sport, he has steadily climbed the ranks. His speed, agility, and ball control have always been his strengths, but the missing piece was his ability to find the back of the net consistently.

### Recent Performances
Recent matches have seen a significant shift in Doku's performance, with him scoring crucial goals for his team. This change in form has not only elevated his team's standings but has also brought him into the spotlight. Fans, critics, and fellow players are now taking notice of his capabilities, sparking discussions about his potential as a top-tier winger.

## Comparison with the Best
To be considered among the best, Doku must be compared to the likes of Mohamed Salah, Kylian Mbappé, and other elite wingers. These players are not only prolific goal-scorers but also consistently perform at a high level, influencing the outcome of matches. Doku's recent goal-scoring streak, while impressive, needs to be sustained over a longer period to be truly comparable to these legends.

### Tactical Impact
Beyond the goals, Doku's tactical awareness and ability to create scoring opportunities for his teammates are crucial aspects of his game. His vision on the field, coupled with his speed, makes him a nightmare for defenders. As he continues to develop his game, his impact on the team's overall strategy and success will be significant.

## The Future Ahead
The football world is eagerly watching Jeremy Doku's next moves. Will he continue to score and lead his team to victory, or will he face a slump? The answer to this question will determine his place among the world's best wingers. For now, his hard work and dedication have certainly made him a contender, and fans can only anticipate what the future holds for this talented young player.

### Conclusion
Jeremy Doku's statement about needing to add more goals to his game was not just a reflection of his personal ambition but a challenge to himself. His recent performances have shown that he is indeed capable of rising to this challenge. As the football season progresses, all eyes will be on Doku to see if he can maintain his current form and cement his position as one of the world's top wingers.
