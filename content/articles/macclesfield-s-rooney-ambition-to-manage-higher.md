Macclesfield's manager, John Rooney, has been making waves in the soccer world with his clear ambition to manage at the highest level possible. In a recent interview, Rooney expressed his desire to take his managerial career to new heights, but with an interesting twist - he wants to achieve this with his current team, Macclesfield.

## Background
John Rooney, brother of the renowned Wayne Rooney, has been making a name for himself in the soccer world. With a successful playing career behind him, Rooney has transitioned into management, taking the reins at Macclesfield. His passion and dedication to the sport are evident, and his ambition to manage at the highest level is a testament to his drive and commitment.

### Ambition to Manage Higher
Rooney's ambition to manage at the highest level is not just a fleeting dream; it's a well-thought-out goal. He believes that with hard work, determination, and the right support, he can take Macclesfield to the top tiers of English soccer. His vision for the team is clear, and he's eager to put in the effort required to make it a reality.

## Commitment to Macclesfield
What's interesting about Rooney's ambition is his commitment to Macclesfield. Unlike many managers who might jump ship at the first opportunity, Rooney wants to take Macclesfield on this journey with him. He's invested in the team and the community, and he believes that together, they can achieve great things.

The road ahead won't be easy, and Rooney is aware of the challenges that lie in wait. However, he's confident in his abilities and those of his team. With a solid plan in place, Rooney is ready to put in the work required to take Macclesfield to the next level.

John Rooney's ambition to manage at the highest level is inspiring, and his commitment to Macclesfield is a testament to his character.
