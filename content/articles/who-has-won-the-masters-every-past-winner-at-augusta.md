# Introduction to The Masters
The Masters, held annually at Augusta National Golf Club, is one of the most revered tournaments in golf. It is the first major of the year and attracts the world's top golfers. The tournament's rich history is filled with memorable moments and legendary winners. In this article, we will delve into the list of past winners, exploring their achievements and the significance of their victories.

## Early Years of The Masters
The first Masters tournament was held in 1934, and since then, it has become a staple of the golf calendar. The tournament has seen its fair share of dominant players and surprise winners. From the early years, players like Horton Smith and Byron Nelson set the tone for what it means to be a Masters champion.

### Notable Winners
Over the years, The Masters has been won by some of the biggest names in golf. Players like Jack Nicklaus, Arnold Palmer, and Gary Player have multiple wins under their belts. These golfing legends have not only left their mark on the tournament but have also inspired generations of golfers.

## Modern Era Winners
In recent years, The Masters has continued to produce thrilling tournaments with unexpected winners.
