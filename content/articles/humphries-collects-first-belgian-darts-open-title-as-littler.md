# Belgian Darts Open Sees New Champion as Favorites Fall
The Belgian Darts Open has concluded with Luke Humphries claiming his first stage title since September. In a thrilling finale, Humphries defeated Jonny Clayton 8-6, silencing critics and proving his skill on the oche.

## Humphries' Road to Victory
Humphries' path to the title was not an easy one. He faced stiff competition throughout the tournament, but his determination and focus saw him through to the final. His victory over Clayton marked a significant milestone in his career, showcasing his growth and improvement as a player.

### Littler's Shock Defeat
Meanwhile, Luke Littler's hopes of a hat-trick at the Belgian Darts Open were dashed in a shock defeat to Niels Zonneveld. Littler had been on a 12-match winning streak, making his loss all the more unexpected. This upset has sent ripples through the darts community, with many left wondering what went wrong for the previously unbeaten Littler.
