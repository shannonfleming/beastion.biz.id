## Introduction
The 2026 World Cup is just around the corner, and with it comes a wave of excitement and anticipation from football fans around the globe. However, amidst the excitement, a campaign has been launched to deliver the 'biggest complaint Fifa has ever received', calling for an investigation into Fifa president Gianni Infantino.
## The Campaign
The campaign, dubbed 'Reboot Fifa', is a class action-style complaint that aims to hold Fifa and its president accountable for various issues that have been plaguing the organization. With fans concerned over safety and the cost of tickets at the tournament, and complaints ongoing against Fifa from human rights organizations and football competitions, the campaign is seeking to bring about change.
### Key Issues
Some of the key issues that the campaign is highlighting include:
* Safety concerns for fans attending the World Cup
* The high cost of tickets, making it difficult for many fans to attend
* Human rights abuses and complaints from organizations and competitions

## The Complaint
The complaint, which will be sent to Fifa's ethics committee after the World Cup, is calling for a thorough investigation into Gianni Infantino's presidency. The campaign is seeking to hold him accountable for the various issues that have arisen during his tenure, including the handling of human rights abuses and the treatment of football competitions.
### What's Next
The campaign is set to send the complaint to Fifa's ethics committee after the World Cup, and it will be interesting to see how the organization responds. With the World Cup just around the corner, the campaign is hoping to bring attention to the issues and push for change.
