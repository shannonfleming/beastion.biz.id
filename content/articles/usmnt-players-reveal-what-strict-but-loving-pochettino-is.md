The US Men's National Team (USMNT) has undergone a significant transformation under the guidance of coach Mauricio Pochettino. In an exclusive interview with ESPN, players have revealed the 'strict' yet 'loving' nature of their coach, shedding light on the cultural shift within the team.

Players described the previous USMNT setup as 'inmates running the asylum,' suggesting a lack of discipline and direction. However, with Pochettino at the helm, the team has experienced a drastic change in culture and approach.

Pochettino's coaching style is characterized by a unique blend of discipline and empathy. He is known for being 'strict' in his expectations, pushing players to their limits, and demanding the highest level of commitment. Yet, he also shows a 'loving' side, taking a genuine interest in the well-being and development of each player.

Players have praised Pochettino's ability to create a sense of unity and purpose within the team.
