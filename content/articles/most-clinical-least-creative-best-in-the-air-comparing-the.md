# Introduction
The World Cup semi-finals are just around the corner, and the four remaining teams - France, Spain, Argentina, and England - are gearing up for their respective matches. As we approach the business end of the tournament, BBC Sport has compiled a statistical breakdown of each team's performance so far.

## Clinical Finishing
When it comes to clinical finishing, France has been the most impressive team in the tournament. With a conversion rate of 21.4%, they have managed to score 11 goals from just 51 shots on target. Spain, on the other hand, has struggled to find the back of the net, with a conversion rate of just 12.5%. Argentina has been decent in front of goal, with a conversion rate of 17.1%, while England has been average, with a conversion rate of 15.4%.

## Creativity
In terms of creativity, Spain has been the most impressive team, with an average of 18.5 chances created per match. France has been decent, with an average of 14.5 chances created per match, while Argentina has struggled to create clear-cut opportunities, with an average of just 12.3 chances created per match. England has been the least creative team, with an average of just 11.8 chances created per match.

## Aerial Ability
When it comes to aerial ability, England has been the most dominant team, with a success rate of 55.6% in aerial duels. France has been decent, with a success rate of 52.2%, while Argentina has struggled, with a success rate of just 46.2%. Spain has been the worst team in the air, with a success rate of just 44.1%.

## Passing Range
In terms of passing range, Spain has been the most impressive team, with an average of 433.5 passes completed per match. France has been decent, with an average of 384.5 passes completed per match, while Argentina has been average, with an average of 364.8 passes completed per match. England has been the worst team in terms of passing range, with an average of just 346.2 passes completed per match.

## Conclusion
As the four semi-finalists prepare to meet, it's clear that each team has its strengths and weaknesses. France has been the most clinical team in front of goal, while Spain has been the most creative team. England has been dominant in the air, while Argentina has struggled to create clear-cut opportunities. With just two matches left to play, it will be interesting to see which team emerges victorious and lifts the World Cup trophy.
