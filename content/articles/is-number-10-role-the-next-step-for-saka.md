The world of football is constantly evolving, with managers and players continually seeking to improve and adapt to the ever-changing landscape of the sport. ## The Role of a Number 10
The number 10 role is a position that has become synonymous with creativity and flair in football. Typically played by a skilled and visionary player, the number 10 is responsible for creating scoring opportunities for their teammates, while also possessing the ability to score goals themselves. This role requires a unique blend of technical ability, tactical awareness, and vision, making it one of the most challenging and rewarding positions on the pitch.

Bukayo Saka has already established himself as one of the most exciting young players in the Premier League. However, with Arteta's potential plan to utilize Saka as a number 10, the young player may be on the verge of taking his game to the next level.

Playing Saka as a number 10 could bring numerous benefits to the Arsenal attack.
