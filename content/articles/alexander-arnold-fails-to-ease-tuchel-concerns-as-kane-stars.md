# Introduction
England's recent match has left manager Thomas Tuchel with more questions than answers. Despite a promising performance from Harry Kane, the team's overall display has raised concerns about the squad's depth and player selection. One player under intense scrutiny is Trent Alexander-Arnold, whose performance has failed to ease Tuchel's doubts.

## Alexander-Arnold's Struggles
Trent Alexander-Arnold, once considered one of England's most promising young players, has struggled to find his footing in recent matches. His defensive vulnerabilities and lack of consistency have raised concerns about his ability to perform at the highest level. Against a strong opponent, Alexander-Arnold's struggles were evident, and his inability to contain the opposition's attack has left Tuchel searching for alternative options.

## Kane's Brilliance
On the other hand, Harry Kane has been a shining light for England, consistently delivering impressive performances. His goal-scoring record is impressive, and his work rate is unparalleled. Kane's ability to lead the line and create scoring opportunities has been a beacon of hope for England, and his presence on the pitch is a significant factor in the team's success.

## Tuchel's Dilemma
Thomas Tuchel faces a difficult decision in the coming weeks. With Alexander-Arnold's form a concern and Kane's importance to the team evident, the manager must weigh his options carefully. Does he stick with Alexander-Arnold, hoping the young player can regain his form, or does he explore alternative options to strengthen the team's defense? The answer to this question will have a significant impact on England's chances of success in the future.

## Conclusion
England's recent match has highlighted the team's strengths and weaknesses. While Harry Kane's performance has been a positive, Trent Alexander-Arnold's struggles have raised concerns about the team's depth. As Thomas Tuchel navigates the challenges ahead, he must make tough decisions about player selection and tactics. One thing is certain, however: England's success will depend on the team's ability to address its weaknesses and build on its strengths. With the right approach, England can overcome its current concerns and achieve greatness in the world of football.
