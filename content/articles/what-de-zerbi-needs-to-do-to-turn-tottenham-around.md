# Introduction
Tottenham Hotspur, a team once considered a top-four contender in the Premier League, finds itself in a precarious position. With the appointment of Roberto De Zerbi as their new manager, the question on everyone's mind is: what does he need to do to turn Tottenham around and ensure their stay in the Premier League? This article delves into the current state of the team and explores the strategies De Zerbi might employ to change their fortunes.

## The Current State of Tottenham
Tottenham's descent into the lower echelons of the Premier League table has been nothing short of astonishing. A combination of factors, including inconsistent performances, defensive frailties, and an inability to capitalize on scoring opportunities, has contributed to their current predicament. The team's morale is at an all-time low, and the pressure to deliver is mounting.

## The Appointment of Roberto De Zerbi
The appointment of Roberto De Zerbi as the new manager of Tottenham marks a significant turning point for the club. De Zerbi, known for his tactical acumen and ability to extract the best from his players, faces a Herculean task in revitalizing a team on the brink of disaster. His experience in managing teams in similar situations will be invaluable in the coming weeks and months.

## Steps to Turn Tottenham Around
So, what can De Zerbi do to turn Tottenham's fortunes around? Several key steps can be taken to address the team's current woes:

1. **Stabilize the Defense**: Tottenham's defensive record has been woeful this season. De Zerbi must work on shoring up the defense, possibly by adopting a more conservative approach and focusing on set pieces, where the team has been particularly vulnerable.
2. **Improve Midfield Cohesion**: The midfield has been a significant area of concern for Tottenham, with a lack of creativity and control hindering the team's ability to dictate the tempo of games. De Zerbi may need to tinker with his midfield lineup and tactics to find a balance between defense and attack.
3. **Rejuvenate the Attack**: Tottenham's attack, once a potent force, has been lackluster this season. De Zerbi must find a way to get the best out of his attacking players, possibly by changing the team's formation or playing style to suit their strengths.
4. **Boost Morale**: The team's morale is at a low ebb, and De Zerbi's man-management skills will be crucial in lifting the players' spirits. He must create a positive and supportive team environment, where players feel valued and motivated to perform at their best.

## Conclusion
Turning Tottenham around will not be an easy task for Roberto De Zerbi. However, with his experience, tactical nous, and man-management skills, he has the tools to address the team's current issues and steer them towards safety. It will require a collective effort from the players, staff, and fans, but with the right approach, Tottenham can navigate this difficult period and emerge stronger on the other side.
