# Pep Guardiola Condemns Fans for Booing Muslim Players Breaking Ramadan Fast

Pep Guardiola has called on football supporters to 'respect religion and diversity' after a section of fans booed a brief stoppage in play at Elland Road to allow Muslim players to break their Ramadan fast. The incident occurred during Manchester City's 1-0 win against Leeds, when play was halted in the 13th minute after the sun had set to allow the Muslim players to eat.

## The Incident

Despite a clear message on the big screen, fans audibly jeered the situation, prompting Guardiola to condemn their behavior. The Manchester City coach emphasized the importance of respecting the players' faith and the diversity of the sport. 'Respect the religion, respect the diversity,' Guardiola said.

## Reaction from Leeds Coach

The Leeds coach also expressed disappointment at the fans' behavior, stating that 'it's disappointing... we need to do better.' The coach's comments reflect the need for greater understanding and respect for the faith and traditions of all players, regardless of their background.
