# Fury Demands Joshua Fight: 'Now or Never'
The boxing world is abuzz with the latest development in the Tyson Fury and Anthony Joshua saga. In a recent statement, Fury condemned Joshua's evasive response to his call-out, saying 'Let's get it on. Now or never!'

## The Background
The highly anticipated matchup between Fury and Joshua has been in the works for years, with both fighters trading verbal jabs and negotiating contracts. However, the latest exchange between the two has brought the situation to a head.

## Fury's Call-Out
Fury, the current WBC heavyweight champion, has been vocal about his desire to face Joshua in the ring. In a recent interview, he expressed his frustration with Joshua's reluctance to commit to a fight, saying 'I'm tired of waiting for him to make up his mind.'

## Joshua's Response
Joshua, the former unified heavyweight champion, has been cautious in his response to Fury's call-out. While he has expressed interest in fighting Fury, he has also been non-committal about the specifics of the matchup.

## Fury's Frustration
Fury's frustration with Joshua's evasive response is palpable. In a statement released on social media, he said 'Let's get it on. Now or never! I'm not going to wait around for him to make up his mind. If he wants to fight, let's do it now.'

## What's Next?
The ball is now in Joshua's court. Will he accept Fury's challenge and commit to a fight, or will he continue to play it safe? The boxing world is waiting with bated breath for Joshua's response.

## The Stakes
The stakes are high for both fighters. A win for either Fury or Joshua would cement their status as the best heavyweight boxer in the world. A loss, on the other hand, would be a significant setback for their careers.

## The Fans
The fans are eager to see the two fighters face off in the ring. The matchup between Fury and Joshua has the potential to be one of the biggest and most lucrative fights in boxing history.

## Conclusion
The situation between Fury and Joshua is coming to a head. Fury's demand for a fight has put the pressure on Joshua to respond. Will Joshua accept the challenge and commit to a fight, or will he continue to play it safe? The boxing world is waiting to find out.
