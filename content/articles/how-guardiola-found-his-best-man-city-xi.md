Manchester City, under the guidance of Pep Guardiola, has been a dominant force in the Premier League. However, their journey to success has not been without its challenges. At one point, it seemed as though the title might elude them, but they have finished the season strongly. In this article, we will explore how Guardiola found his best Manchester City XI.

When Guardiola first arrived at Manchester City, he was faced with the task of transforming a team that had become stagnant. This period of experimentation was crucial in understanding the strengths and weaknesses of his players.

One of the key factors in Guardiola's success has been his ability to adapt. He has been willing to change his system to suit the players at his disposal, as well as the opposition they are facing.
