**Aston Martin's Formula 1 team is bracing for a challenging start to the 2026 season due to their late commencement on the new car.**

## Context / Background
Aston Martin's Chief Technical Officer, Adrian Newey, has expressed concerns over the team's late start on their 2026 Formula 1 challenger. This delay is expected to have a significant impact on the team's performance in the early stages of the season.

## Tactical Key Points / Stats Table
The late start will likely hinder the team's ability to test and refine their car, potentially leading to a slow start to the season. While specific details about the car's design and performance are not yet available, it is clear that the team faces an uphill battle.

> Adrian Newey admitted that the late start is 'bound to' impact the team's results in the early stages of the new season.
