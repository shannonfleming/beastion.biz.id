# Introduction to the Managerial Search
Crystal Palace, the recent Conference League winners, are currently in the midst of a significant managerial search. However, with the process taking longer than anticipated, alternative options are being explored, with Coventry boss Frank Lampard emerging as a potential plan B.

Andoni Iraola, known for his impressive managerial career, has been the focus of Crystal Palace's attention for some time. His experience and tactical prowess make him an attractive candidate for the role. However, the delay in his response has prompted the club to consider other viable options.

Frank Lampard, the current manager of Coventry, has been identified as a potential alternative should Iraola decline the offer or if the negotiations fail to reach a positive conclusion. The prospect of Lampard at the helm of Crystal Palace is intriguing, considering his potential to adapt to the club's ambitions and squad dynamics.
