# Introduction
England's recent success over Spain has brought attention to the team's talented roster, with Lauren James being one of the standout players. However, the question remains: how can Sarina Wiegman get the best from one of England's most talented footballers?

## Lauren James' Profile
Lauren James is a versatile and skilled player who can play in various positions across the front line. Her ability to score goals, create chances, and beat opponents with her pace and skill makes her a valuable asset to the team.

### Strengths
- **Goal-scoring ability**: James has consistently shown her ability to score goals from various positions on the pitch.
- **Creativity**: She possesses excellent vision and passing range, allowing her to create scoring opportunities for her teammates.
- **Pace and skill**: James' speed and agility enable her to beat opponents and create space for herself and others.

## Tactical Analysis
To unlock James' full potential, Wiegman must consider the following tactical adjustments:

1. **Positioning**: Experimenting with James in different positions across the front line could help identify her most effective role. This could include playing her as a center-forward, winger, or even in a more advanced midfield role.
2. **Freedom to roam**: Allowing James the freedom to roam across the front line and find space could help her exploit defensive weaknesses and create scoring opportunities.
3. **Playing to her strengths**: Wiegman should look to play to James' strengths by providing her with opportunities to use her pace and skill to beat opponents.

## Case Study: Recent Success over Spain
In England's recent success over Spain, James played a crucial role in the team's attack. Her ability to create chances and score goals was evident, and her pace and skill caused problems for the Spanish defense throughout the match.

## Conclusion
Getting the best from Lauren James will be crucial to England's success in upcoming tournaments. By understanding her strengths and weaknesses, and making tactical adjustments to accommodate her skills, Sarina Wiegman can help James reach her full potential and become a key player for the team.
