## France Shifts Gears to Overcome Paraguay in the World Cup
France kept the champagne football on ice in their recent match against Paraguay, instead opting for a more pragmatic approach to secure a 1-0 victory. The World Cup favorites were forced to drop a gear or two due to the sweltering heat in Philadelphia, but still managed to outmaneuver their opponents.

### A Different Approach
The match was a far cry from the thrilling encounters of the past, such as the 1958 World Cup where Just Fontaine scored a hat-trick against Sweden. This time around, France's goal machine, Kylian Mbappé, had to settle for just one goal, a penalty that drew him level with Lionel Messi in the Golden Boot race.

### The Winning Goal
Mbappé's penalty was the deciding factor in the match, and it was a testament to his skill and composure under pressure. The goal also brought him one step closer to breaking the all-time goalscoring record, currently held by Lionel Messi.

### A Grueling Match
The match was a grueling affair, with both teams struggling to cope with the heat. Paraguay set out to make life difficult for France, and they succeeded in making the match a physical and intense encounter. However, France's experience and skill ultimately told, as they managed to grind out a hard-fought victory.

### The Road Ahead
The win sets France up for a promising run in the World Cup, and they will be looking to build on this momentum in their upcoming matches. With Mbappé in top form, they will be a force to be reckoned with, and their opponents will need to be at the top of their game to stand a chance.

### Conclusion
In conclusion, France's victory over Paraguay was a testament to their ability to adapt and overcome adversity. The team's willingness to ditch their usual 'tuxedos' and play 'dirty football' was a key factor in their success, and they will be looking to repeat this formula in their upcoming matches. With the World Cup heating up, France is definitely a team to watch, and their fans will be eagerly anticipating their next match.
