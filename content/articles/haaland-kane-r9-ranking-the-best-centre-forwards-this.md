# Introduction
The 21st century has witnessed some of the most exceptional centre-forwards in football history. From the clinical finishing of Robert Lewandowski to the sheer power of Erling Haaland, the modern era has been blessed with an abundance of talented strikers. In this article, we will attempt to rank the best centre-forwards of the 21st century, considering their goalscoring records, overall impact on the game, and dominance on the pitch.

## The Contenders
The list of top centre-forwards is extensive, but some names stand out from the rest. Harry Kane, with his consistent goal-scoring form for both Tottenham Hotspur and England, is a strong contender. The Brazilian legend, Ronaldo (R9), is another player who deserves consideration, given his impressive record in the early 2000s. Other notable mentions include Zlatan Ibrahimovic, Karim Benzema, and Sergio Aguero, all of whom have made significant contributions to their respective teams.

### The Top 5
After careful analysis, we have narrowed down the list to the top 5 centre-forwards of the 21st century.

1. **Erling Haaland**: The Norwegian sensation has taken the football world by storm with his incredible goal-scoring record. His pace, power, and finishing ability make him a nightmare for defenders to deal with.
2. **Robert Lewandowski**: The Polish striker has been one of the most consistent goal-scorers of the century, with an impressive record in both the Bundesliga and the Champions League.
3. **Harry Kane**: The English striker has established himself as one of the top centre-forwards in the world, with a remarkable goal-scoring record for both club and country.
4. **Ronaldo (R9)**: The Brazilian legend is widely regarded as one of the greatest centre-forwards of all time, with an impressive record in the early 2000s.
5. **Zlatan Ibrahimovic**: The Swedish striker has had a remarkable career, with a plethora of goals and trophies to his name, and is widely regarded as one of the most talented centre-forwards of the century.

## Conclusion
Ranking the best centre-forwards of the 21st century is a challenging task, given the numerous talented strikers who have graced the pitch.
