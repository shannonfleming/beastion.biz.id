# Introduction
The United States men's national team (USMNT) has come a long way since their embarrassing performance in the 1990 World Cup in Italy. With the World Cup returning to American soil in 2026, it's an opportune time to reflect on the team's transformation and the factors that contributed to their growth.

## A Humbling Experience in Italy
The 1990 World Cup in Italy was a disappointing tournament for the USMNT. They were eliminated in the group stage, losing all three matches and conceding 8 goals in the process. The team's performance was widely criticized, and it seemed like a long shot for the US to become a competitive force in international soccer.

## The Turning Point: USA 1994
However, the 1994 World Cup, hosted by the United States, marked a turning point for the USMNT. The team, led by coach Bora Milutinovic, showed significant improvement, reaching the round of 16 before being eliminated by Brazil. The tournament's success, both on and off the field, helped to galvanize interest in soccer in the United States and laid the foundation for the team's future growth.

### Key Factors in the USMNT's Transformation
Several factors have contributed to the USMNT's transformation into a competitive force in international soccer:
* **Investment in Youth Development**: The US Soccer Federation has invested heavily in youth development programs, providing young players with access to top-level coaching, training facilities, and competition.
* **MLS and Professional Leagues**: The establishment of Major League Soccer (MLS) in 1996 has provided a professional league for American players to develop and compete in. The growth of MLS has also led to an increase in the number of American players competing in top European leagues.
* **International Competition**: The USMNT's participation in international competitions, such as the World Cup and the CONCACAF Gold Cup, has helped the team gain valuable experience and exposure to different playing styles and opponents.

## The Road to 2026
As the USMNT prepares to host the 2026 World Cup, the team is poised to make a deep run in the tournament. With a talented young core, led by players like Christian Pulisic and Giovanni Reyna, the USMNT has the potential to make a significant impact on the international stage.

### Challenges Ahead
Despite the team's progress, there are still challenges to be addressed. The USMNT faces stiff competition from other top national teams, and the team's consistency and depth will be tested in the lead-up to the 2026 World Cup.

## Conclusion
The USMNT's transformation from embarrassment in Italy to a World Cup force is a testament to the team's hard work, dedication, and the investment in youth development and professional leagues. As the team prepares to host the 2026 World Cup, American soccer fans have reason to be optimistic about the USMNT's chances of making a deep run in the tournament.
