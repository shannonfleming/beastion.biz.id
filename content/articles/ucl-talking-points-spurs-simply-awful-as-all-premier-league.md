# Introduction
The first leg of the Champions League has come to a close, and it's been a disappointing week for Premier League teams. All English teams failed to win their respective matches, with some putting in particularly lackluster performances. Tottenham Hotspur's 1-0 loss to AC Milan was perhaps the most disappointing result of the week. ## Other Premier League Teams Struggle
It wasn't just Spurs who struggled, however. All Premier League teams failed to win their first-leg matches, with some putting in particularly uninspiring performances. Liverpool's 2-2 draw with Atletico Madrid was a more exciting affair, but the team's defensive issues were once again on display.

## Looking Ahead to the Return Fixtures
