# Lionel Messi Makes History: Argentina Beats Austria in World Cup

Lionel Messi has once again proven why he is considered one of the greatest football players of all time. In a match against Austria in the World Cup, Messi broke the record for most goals scored in the tournament, solidifying his position as the highest goalscorer in World Cup history.

## The Match

The match between Argentina and Austria was an intense and highly anticipated one. From the start, it was clear that Messi was on a mission to make history. With his incredible skills and determination, he led his team to victory, scoring not one, but two crucial goals.

### The Record-Breaking Goal

The first goal came late in the first half, with Messi showcasing his signature left-footed flourish. This goal marked his 17th in the World Cup, but he wasn't done yet. In the dying minutes of the match, Messi scored again, this time securing his 18th goal and breaking the record for most goals scored in the World Cup.
