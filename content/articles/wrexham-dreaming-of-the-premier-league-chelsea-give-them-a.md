However, as their FA Cup loss to Chelsea shows, they still have a long way to go.

### The FA Cup Loss to Chelsea
The FA Cup loss to Chelsea was a sobering experience for Wrexham. Despite their best efforts, they were unable to match the strength and skill of the Premier League giants. The loss serves as a reminder of the huge gap between the National League and the Premier League. ## The Challenges of the Premier League
