Rory McIlroy, one of the most skilled golfers of his generation, has been dealing with inconsistencies in his game. The range session, a staple of golfers looking to fine-tune their skills, has historically been a place where McIlroy finds solace and improvement. By dedicating time to understand and correct the bad habits that have crept into his game, McIlroy aims to 'straighten out' his waywardness. During his title defense at The Masters, a similar range session proved to be a pivotal moment for McIlroy. As the golfing world sets its sights on the PGA Championship, all eyes are on McIlroy. Given his past achievements and the importance he places on these practice sessions, there's a strong belief that McIlroy could indeed find himself back in the running for a major title.

By taking proactive steps to improve, McIlroy not only works on his technique but also reinforces his belief in his abilities, a crucial aspect of competing at the highest level.
