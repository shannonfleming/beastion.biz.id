# Premier League and FA Cup Final: A Weekend of High Stakes
The Premier League and FA Cup final are set to take center stage this weekend, with several high-profile matches that could have a significant impact on the season. In this article, we will take a look at the top 10 things to watch out for in the Premier League and FA Cup final.

## FA Cup Final: Chelsea vs Manchester City
The FA Cup final between Chelsea and Manchester City is one of the most highly anticipated matches of the weekend. Pep Guardiola's Manchester City will be looking to claim their 17th major trophy under his leadership, while Chelsea will be seeking to cause an upset. With Guardiola potentially departing in the close season, this match could be one of his last in charge of Manchester City.

## A Measure of Greatness
Pep Guardiola's greatness can be measured by his impressive record at Manchester City. With 24 visits to Wembley, he has established himself as one of the most successful managers in the club's history. However, Chelsea are not to be underestimated, and their new caretaker manager, Calum McFarlane, will be looking to make a statement.

## Premier League Matches
In addition to the FA Cup final, there are several Premier League matches taking place this weekend. Aston Villa will face Liverpool on Friday, while Manchester United will take on Nottingham Forest on Sunday. Brentford will face Crystal Palace, and Everton will face Sunderland, in matches that could have a significant impact on the European qualification spots.

## European Dreams
Brentford's match against Crystal Palace could be a crucial one in their quest for European qualification. A win for Brentford could edge them closer to a European spot, while a loss could make it difficult for them to catch up. With several teams still in contention for European qualification, this match is one to watch.

## Conclusion
The Premier League and FA Cup final are set to be an exciting and unpredictable weekend of football. With several high-profile matches and high stakes, there is a lot to look forward to. Whether you are a fan of Manchester City, Chelsea, or one of the other teams in action, this weekend is sure to be an exciting one.
