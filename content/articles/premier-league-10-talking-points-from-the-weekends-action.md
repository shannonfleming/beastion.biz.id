# Premier League Analysis: A Weekend of Highs and Lows
The Premier League weekend has come and gone, leaving us with plenty to discuss and analyze. From Tottenham's dismal performance to the ongoing battle for England's deputy striker spot, there's no shortage of talking points.

## Tottenham's Struggles Continue
Tottenham's woes continued over the weekend, with the team looking devoid of energy, creativity, and quality. Their lackluster performance has left them in serious danger of relegation, with their upcoming fixtures offering little respite. The injury list hasn't helped, but it's clear that manager Igor Tudor needs to rethink his strategy if he hopes to save the season.

One possible solution could be to play both Dominic Solanke and Randal Kolo Muani, getting balls into the box and hoping they can make enough of them to secure vital points. While this may not be the most elegant approach, it's certainly better than the alternative – relegation.

## The Battle for England's Deputy Striker Spot
Meanwhile, the tussle to become Harry Kane's England deputy continues to heat up. With several players vying for the spot, it's anyone's game. Rio Ngumoha's impressive performance for Liverpool has certainly thrown his hat into the ring, but other contenders are also making their cases.

## Chelsea's Self-Destruction
Chelsea's self-destructive tendencies were on full display over the weekend, with the team's lack of discipline and composure costing them dearly. It's a problem that needs to be addressed if they hope to make a push for the top spots.

## West Ham and Nottingham Forest on the Rise
On a more positive note, West Ham and Nottingham Forest are both playing well, with their form making them dark horses in the relegation battle. Spurs, on the other hand, haven't won a league game in 2026 and look like they've forgotten how.

## Conclusion
The Premier League is never short on drama, and this weekend was no exception. With Tottenham's struggles, the battle for England's deputy striker spot, and Chelsea's self-destruction, there's plenty to talk about. As the season heats up, it will be interesting to see how these storylines play out and which teams will emerge victorious.
