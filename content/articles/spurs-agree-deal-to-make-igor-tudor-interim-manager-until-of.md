# Tottenham Appoint Igor Tudor as Interim Manager Until End of Season
Tottenham have reached an agreement for Igor Tudor to become their interim manager until the end of the season, leaving the door open for Mauricio Pochettino to return this summer.

## Background on Igor Tudor
Igor Tudor, a former Croatia international, has been out of work since he left Juventus in October after the Serie A club went eight games without a win. His former clubs include Juventus and Marseille, showcasing his experience in managing top-tier teams.

## The Appointment Process
The club turned to Tudor after making checks on the former Borussia Dortmund manager Edin Terzic and the former RB Leipzig manager Marco Rose. This thorough process indicates that Tottenham were meticulous in their search for an interim manager.

## Implications of the Appointment
The appointment of Tudor as interim manager until the end of the season has significant implications. Firstly, it provides stability to the team for the remainder of the season, allowing them to focus on their league performance without the distraction of a managerial vacuum. Secondly, it leaves the door open for Mauricio Pochettino to return in the summer, which could be a strategic move to attract a high-profile manager.

## Potential for Pochettino's Return
The fact that the door remains open for Mauricio Pochettino to return this summer suggests that Tottenham are keeping their options open for the long term. Pochettino's potential return could be a significant boost to the team, given his previous success with the club.

## Challenges Ahead for Tudor
Igor Tudor faces significant challenges as he takes the reins at Tottenham. He will need to quickly adapt to the team's dynamics and implement his own strategies to achieve success. The pressure to perform will be high, especially given the expectations surrounding the team.

## Conclusion
In conclusion, the appointment of Igor Tudor as interim manager until the end of the season is a strategic move by Tottenham. It provides stability for the remainder of the season while keeping the door open for potential long-term changes. As the season unfolds, it will be interesting to see how Tudor navigates the challenges ahead and whether Pochettino will eventually return to the club.
