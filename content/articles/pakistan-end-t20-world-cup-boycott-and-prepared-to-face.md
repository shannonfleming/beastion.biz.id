**Pakistan has ended their proposed boycott of the T20 World Cup match against India, confirming they will play as scheduled this Sunday.**
## Context / Background
The T20 World Cup has been a platform for intense rivalries, and the match between Pakistan and India is one of the most highly anticipated. The initial proposal to boycott the match had sent shockwaves through the cricket community.

## Tactical Key Points
The decision to end the boycott comes after Bangladesh's removal from the tournament, potentially altering the dynamics of the competition. 

> No specific quotes have been provided regarding the decision to end the boycott.

## What's Next?
The match between Pakistan and India is scheduled for this Sunday and will be broadcast live on Sky Sports. Fans are eagerly awaiting the encounter, which promises to be a thrilling contest between two cricketing powerhouses.
