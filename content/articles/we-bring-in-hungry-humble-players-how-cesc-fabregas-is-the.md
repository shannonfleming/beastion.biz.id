# Introduction
The serene sound of lapping water is broken only by the whir of a seaplane engine swooping to land. Outside the hangar to which the aircraft will soon return, a crowd has gathered well before kick-off. Later, most will scale the steep steps of the adjacent Stadio Giuseppe Sinigaglia. This unique setting is home to Como 1907, a tiny Italian club that is making waves in the football world.

## The Unique Setting
To the right of the stadium sits Villa Carminati, a new private members’ club that also houses hospitality guests on matchdays. It is a unique setting for a unique football club. When bathed in sunshine, as was the case for last Sunday’s goalless draw with Napoli, Como 1907 is a waterside paradise. Or, as their president Mirwan Suwarso puts it when drawing parallels between the club and Disney, it is the “theme park” division of the “main brand … Lake Como”.

### Cesc Fàbregas: The Leader
At the heart of Como 1907’s rise is Cesc Fàbregas, the former Arsenal player who has brought his expertise and experience to the club. Fàbregas has been instrumental in shaping the team’s playing style and has been praised for his leadership on and off the pitch. His philosophy of bringing in “hungry, humble players” has been key to the club’s success.

#### The Scouting Guru
Behind the scenes, a scouting guru inspired by Football Manager has been working tirelessly to identify and recruit talented players. This unique approach has allowed Como 1907 to uncover hidden gems and build a strong squad. The club’s owners have also been ambitious in their recruitment, bringing in players who share the club’s vision and values.

## The Rise of Como 1907
Como 1907’s rise has been nothing short of incredible. From a small club playing in the lower leagues to a team competing against the likes of Napoli, the club has come a long way. The team’s success has been built on a strong foundation of hard work, dedication, and a clear vision for the future.

### The Future of Como 1907
As the club continues to grow and develop, the future looks bright for Como 1907. With Cesc Fàbregas at the helm and a talented squad of players, the club is well-placed to compete at the highest level. The unique setting of the Stadio Giuseppe Sinigaglia and the waterside paradise of Lake Como will continue to inspire the team and attract fans from around the world.

## Conclusion
The rise of Como 1907 is a testament to the power of hard work, dedication, and a clear vision for the future. With Cesc Fàbregas leading the charge, the club is well-placed to continue its upward trajectory. As the team takes to the pitch, the serene sound of lapping water and the whir of the seaplane engine will be replaced by the roar of the crowd, cheering on the team as they strive for greatness.
