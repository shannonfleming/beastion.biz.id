The world of football is known for its unpredictability, and the recent developments at Manchester City have left many fans wondering about the future of their deputy goalkeeper, James Trafford. Trafford rejoined Manchester City on July 31, 2025, in a deal worth £31m after spending two years at Burnley. However, little did he know that his return would be short-lived as the club's first choice. Pep Guardiola, the manager of Manchester City, had other plans, and on September 2, 2025, the club announced the signing of Gianluigi Donnarumma from Paris Saint-Germain for £26m. This move has left Trafford's future at the club uncertain.

Donnarumma's arrival has had a significant impact on Trafford's career at Manchester City. The Italian goalkeeper has become Guardiola's first choice, leaving Trafford to warm the bench. This unexpected turn of events has left Trafford to ponder his future at the club.
