# Introduction to the NWSL 2026 Season
The National Women's Soccer League (NWSL) is set to kick off its 2026 season, promising an exciting year of women's soccer. With 16 teams competing, the league has never been more competitive. In this mega-preview, we'll delve into the key moves, questions, and predictions for each team, highlighting the stars that will shape the season.

## Eastern Conference
The Eastern Conference is packed with talented teams, each with its own strengths and weaknesses. Here's a breakdown of what to expect from each team:

### 1. North Carolina Courage
- **Key Moves:** The Courage have made some significant signings, bolstering their midfield and defense.
- **Questions:** Can they maintain their dominance without some of their key players from last season?
- **Predictions:** Despite the changes, the Courage are expected to be one of the top contenders in the East.

### 2. Washington Spirit
- **Key Moves:** The Spirit have focused on strengthening their attack, bringing in new forwards.
- **Questions:** Will their defense be able to withstand the pressure of the new season?
- **Predictions:** With their enhanced offense, the Spirit are predicted to make a strong push for the playoffs.

### 3. Orlando Pride
- **Key Moves:** Orlando has made several key acquisitions to improve their midfield control.
- **Questions:** Can their new midfielders gel quickly enough to make an impact?
- **Predictions:** The Pride are expected to be in the mix for a playoff spot, depending on how their new signings perform.

## Western Conference
The Western Conference is equally as competitive, with several teams looking to make a deep run into the playoffs. Here's an overview of the Western Conference teams:

### 1. OL Reign
- **Key Moves:** The Reign have bolstered their defense, aiming to reduce the number of goals conceded.
- **Questions:** Will their offense be able to produce enough goals to support their improved defense?
- **Predictions:** With their strengthened defense, OL Reign is a favorite to win the West.

### 2. Portland Thorns FC
- **Key Moves:** Portland has made some strategic signings to enhance their midfield and forward lines.
- **Questions:** Can they maintain consistency throughout the season?
- **Predictions:** The Thorns are predicted to be one of the top teams in the league, with a good chance of making it to the championship final.

### 3. San Diego Wave FC
- **Key Moves:** San Diego has focused on building a strong team dynamic, with several new players joining the squad.
- **Questions:** How quickly can the new players adapt to the team's playing style?
- **Predictions:** The Wave are expected to be a dark horse in the West, potentially surprising some of the more established teams.
