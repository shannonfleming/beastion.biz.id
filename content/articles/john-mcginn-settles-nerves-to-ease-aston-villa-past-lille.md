# Aston Villa Ease Past Lille into Quarter-Finals
Aston Villa have secured their spot in the quarter-finals of the Europa League, thanks to a decisive win over Lille. The match, which was played without much drama, saw John McGinn score the crucial goal that eased the nerves of the Villa fans.

The second leg of the tie was a relatively calm affair, with Villa dominating the proceedings. The team had been missing their Scottish standard bearer, John McGinn, but his return to the lineup made a significant impact. McGinn's goal, which came as a result of a flowing move that took just 15 seconds, was the highlight of the match.

### Key Contributions
While McGinn's goal was the decisive factor, other players also made significant contributions to the team's win.
