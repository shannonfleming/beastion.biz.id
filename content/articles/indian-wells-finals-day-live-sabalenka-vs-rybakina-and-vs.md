The Indian Wells Finals Day has finally arrived, and tennis fans are on the edge of their seats as two thrilling matches are set to unfold. The women's final promises to be an exciting encounter between two of the world's top players. The head-to-head record between Sabalenka and Rybakina is evenly poised, with each player having won two of their four previous meetings. However, Sabalenka's recent form suggests that she may have the upper hand in this encounter.
