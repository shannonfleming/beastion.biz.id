## The Kentucky Derby: A Prestigious Event
The Kentucky Derby, often referred to as the 'Run for the Roses,' is one of the most esteemed events in the horse racing calendar. Held at Churchill Downs in Louisville, Kentucky, it marks the first leg of the Triple Crown of Thoroughbred Racing, followed by the Preakness Stakes and the Belmont Stakes. This year, the event is headlined by Renegade and Commandment, two strong contenders expected to give a thrilling performance.

### Renegade and Commandment: The Favorites
- **Renegade**: Known for its speed and agility, Renegade has been a favorite among racing enthusiasts. Its past performances have shown a consistent ability to lead from the front, making it a strong contender for the Derby.
- **Commandment**: With a powerful running style, Commandment has made a name for itself by overcoming challenging tracks and opponents. Its determination and strength make it a formidable opponent in the race.
