# Tottenham's Season Takes a Hit: Romero Ruled Out with Knee Injury

Romero, who has been a vital part of the team's defense, was forced to leave the game due to the severity of the injury. Initial assessments indicated that the injury was serious, and further tests have confirmed that Romero will not be able to participate in any games for the remainder of the season.
