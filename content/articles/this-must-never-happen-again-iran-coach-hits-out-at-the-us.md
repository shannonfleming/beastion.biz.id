# Iran Coach Demands Action from FIFA President After US Treatment

## Background
Iran is currently waiting to see if they will qualify for the knockout stage, with their fate dependent on the results of other matches. However, their recent draw against Egypt in Seattle has left a sour taste, with the team feeling that they were treated 'very unfairly' by the hosts.

### The Match

## Ghalenoei's Statement
Ghalenoei reiterated his belief that the US has treated his team unfairly, and that FIFA President Gianni Infantino must take action to prevent such incidents in the future. The coach's statement has sparked a heated debate, with many calling on FIFA to investigate the matter and take necessary actions.

### FIFA's Response
FIFA has yet to respond to Ghalenoei's statement, but the organization is under pressure to address the concerns raised by the Iran coach. With the World Cup being a global event, FIFA must ensure that all teams are treated fairly and with respect.

## Conclusion
The incident has highlighted the need for FIFA to take a stronger stance against hosts who do not treat visiting teams with respect and fairness.
