# Introduction
Manchester United is on the cusp of a new era, with the club's senior executives poised to recommend Michael Carrick as the permanent boss. This development comes as a surprise to many, but a closer look at Carrick's credentials and the club's current situation reveals that he might be the ideal candidate for the job.

## Carrick's Background and Experience
Michael Carrick is a Manchester United legend, having played for the club from 2006 to 2018. During his playing career, he won numerous titles, including five Premier League championships, the Champions League, and the FIFA Club World Cup. After retiring as a player, Carrick transitioned into coaching, serving as an assistant to Jose Mourinho and Ole Gunnar Solskjaer. His experience and knowledge of the club's inner workings make him an attractive candidate for the top job.

## The Case for Carrick
Carrick's appointment as permanent boss would be a shrewd move by the club's executives. He has already demonstrated his ability to lead the team, having taken charge as caretaker manager on several occasions. His tactical acumen and man-management skills have earned him the respect of the players, and his familiarity with the club's culture and values would ensure a seamless transition.

## The Challenges Ahead
While Carrick's appointment would be a popular choice among fans, he would still face significant challenges as the permanent boss. The club is currently undergoing a period of transition, with a number of high-profile players departing and new signings expected to arrive. Carrick would need to navigate these changes while also addressing the team's inconsistent form and lack of cohesion on the pitch.

## The Ratcliffe Factor
The impending arrival of Sir Jim Ratcliffe as the club's new owner adds an extra layer of complexity to the situation. Ratcliffe's vision for the club and his expectations for the team's performance would undoubtedly influence Carrick's tenure as manager. Carrick would need to demonstrate his ability to work with the new owner and adapt to his expectations, all while maintaining the trust and confidence of the players and fans.

## Conclusion
Michael Carrick's potential appointment as Manchester United's permanent boss is a tantalizing prospect for fans and pundits alike. With his experience, knowledge, and tactical acumen, he has the potential to succeed in the role. However, he would still face significant challenges, from navigating the club's transition to meeting the expectations of the new owner. As the club's senior executives prepare to recommend Carrick for the job, one thing is certain – the future of Manchester United is about to become a lot more interesting.
