England's World Cup preparations have drawn to a close, and the focus now shifts to the highly anticipated match against Croatia. As Thomas Tuchel prepares his team for this crucial encounter, several key decisions will need to be made regarding the starting lineup.

Bellingham has consistently demonstrated his exceptional skill and versatility on the pitch, making him an attractive option for this position. However, Tuchel must carefully weigh the pros and cons of assigning Bellingham to this role, considering the potential impact on the team's overall performance.

## Tuchel's Tactical Considerations
As Tuchel navigates the complexities of team selection, he will need to consider a range of tactical factors. These include the strengths and weaknesses of the Croatian team, the most effective formations and strategies to employ, and the optimal player combinations to achieve success.
