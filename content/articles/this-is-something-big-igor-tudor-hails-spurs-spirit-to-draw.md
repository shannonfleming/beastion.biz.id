# Tottenham's Resilience Earns First Point Under Igor Tudor
Tottenham Hotspur secured their first point under new head coach Igor Tudor with a late draw against Liverpool. The match, which ended 1-1, saw Richarlison score a last-minute equalizer to deny Liverpool a victory that would have moved them into fourth place.

The match began with Liverpool taking the lead in the first half through a free-kick by Dominik Szoboszlai. However, Tottenham, who had only 12 senior outfield players available, were able to hold on and eventually equalize in the last minute of the game.

### Tudor's Reaction
Igor Tudor described the draw as the start of 'something big' for Tottenham. The Croat had overseen four straight defeats since taking over and was heading for a fifth until Richarlison's late equalizer. Tudor handed Premier League debuts to two teenagers but his side were still able to hold the defending champions.
