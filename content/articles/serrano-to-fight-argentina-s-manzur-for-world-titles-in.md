### Introduction
The world of boxing is set to witness a historic moment as Amanda Serrano prepares to defend her world titles against Lucrecia Manzur on August 21. This highly anticipated fight is not only significant for the competitors involved but also for the platform on which it will be broadcast: TikTok. For the first time, a major boxing event will be live-streamed on the popular social media app, marking a new era in sports broadcasting.

### The Fighters
Amanda Serrano, a renowned figure in women's boxing, has been making waves with her impressive skills and determination in the ring. With a strong record and multiple titles under her belt, Serrano is a force to be reckoned with. Her opponent, Lucrecia Manzur from Argentina, is also a skilled boxer looking to make a statement by challenging one of the best in the sport.

### The Significance of the Event
The decision to broadcast this fight on TikTok is a bold move that reflects the evolving landscape of sports media. TikTok, known for its short-form videos, has been expanding its content offerings to include live events, and this boxing match is a significant step in that direction. It opens up new avenues for sports fans to engage with their favorite events and athletes, potentially reaching a broader and younger audience.

### The Impact on Women's Boxing
This event also highlights the growing recognition and support for women's boxing. The visibility provided by a platform like TikTok can play a crucial role in promoting women's boxing, attracting new fans, and fostering a more inclusive sports community.
