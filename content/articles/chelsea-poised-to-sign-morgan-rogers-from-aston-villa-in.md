# Chelsea Set to Break Records with £117m Signing of Morgan Rogers
Chelsea are poised to sign Morgan Rogers from Aston Villa in a record-breaking deal worth £117m, making him the most expensive English player. The attacking midfielder has agreed terms on a six-year contract and is due to undergo a medical on Monday.

## The Deal
The £117m fee will make Rogers the most expensive English player, surpassing the previous record. Chelsea have long tracked the 23-year-old’s progress and have moved quickly to land one of Xabi Alonso’s top targets.

## Interest from Arsenal
Arsenal were also keen on Rogers, a £15m Villa signing, and made contact with Villa this month. However, Chelsea have beaten them to the signing, securing one of the most promising young players in the English game.

Morgan Rogers is a highly skilled attacking midfielder who has been making waves in the football world. At just 23 years old, he has already shown immense potential and has been identified as a key target by several top clubs.
