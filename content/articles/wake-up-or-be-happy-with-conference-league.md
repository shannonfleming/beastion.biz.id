# Introduction
Liverpool's latest draw with Tottenham has left the Reds reeling, and the consequences of their inability to close out games are beginning to manifest. The 1-1 draw, courtesy of a late goal, has become an all-too-familiar theme for Jurgen Klopp's side this season.

## The Problem: Late Goals
The problem is clear: Liverpool are conceding late goals, and it's costing them valuable points. This is not a new issue, but rather a recurring theme that has plagued the team throughout the season. The question on everyone's mind is: what's causing this trend?

### Analysis
To understand the root of the problem, it's essential to analyze Liverpool's recent performances. The team has shown flashes of brilliance, with impressive victories against top-tier opponents. However, their inability to close out games has been their Achilles' heel. The latest draw with Tottenham is a prime example of this.

#### Tactics and Strategy
From a tactical standpoint, Liverpool's approach has been questioned. The team's high-pressing strategy, which has been a hallmark of Klopp's tenure, has been less effective in recent games. The opposition has found ways to exploit Liverpool's aggressiveness, catching them on the counter and scoring crucial goals.

## The Consequences: Conference League Football
The consequences of Liverpool's late collapses are dire. With the team struggling to close out games, they risk being left behind in the Premier League table. The Reds are currently in a precarious position, and their inability to secure wins could ultimately lead to a disappointing finish. The specter of Conference League football is looming large, and it's a prospect that will send shivers down the spines of Liverpool fans.

### The Solution
So, what's the solution? Liverpool need to address their defensive frailties and find a way to close out games. This may require a tweak in tactics, with a greater emphasis on game management and defensive solidity. The team must also work on their mental toughness, developing the ability to see out games and secure valuable wins.

## Conclusion
In conclusion, Liverpool's late collapse against Tottenham is a wake-up call for the team. The Reds must address their defensive issues and find a way to close out games if they hope to achieve their season goals. The alternative is a bleak one: Conference League football. It's time for Klopp and his team to take a long, hard look at themselves and make the necessary adjustments to ensure a successful season.
