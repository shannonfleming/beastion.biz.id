# Introduction
The 2026 World Cup has been filled with exciting moments, and one of the most magical nights belonged to Lionel Messi and Argentina. However, the night could have taken a drastic turn if not for a VAR review that spared Messi from a potential red card.

## The Incident
The incident in question occurred when Messi was involved in a challenge that could have been interpreted as a reckless tackle. The referee initially did not take any action, but the VAR team reviewed the play and decided that it did not warrant a red card. This decision was met with relief from the Argentine fans, as a red card would have significantly impacted the team's chances of winning.

### VAR Review Process
The VAR review process is designed to help referees make accurate decisions, especially in critical situations. The process involves a team of officials who review the play and provide feedback to the on-field referee. In this case, the VAR team reviewed the challenge and determined that it did not meet the criteria for a red card.

#### Criteria for a Red Card
According to the FIFA rules, a red card is given to a player who commits a serious foul play, violent conduct, or spits at an opponent. The challenge involving Messi did not meet these criteria, and therefore, the VAR team decided not to recommend a red card.

## Impact on the Game
The decision not to give Messi a red card had a significant impact on the game. Argentina was able to maintain their momentum and go on to win the match. If Messi had been given a red card, the team would have been forced to play with one less player, which would have significantly impacted their chances of winning.

### Reaction from Fans and Coaches
The reaction from the fans and coaches was mixed. Some argued that the challenge was reckless and deserved a red card, while others believed that it was a fair tackle. The Argentine coach was relieved that Messi was not given a red card, stating that it would have been a significant blow to the team.

## Conclusion
The VAR review process is an essential part of the game, and it helps to ensure that the referees make accurate decisions. In this case, the VAR team made the correct decision in not recommending a red card for Messi. The decision had a significant impact on the game, and it allowed Argentina to go on and win the match.
