# Introduction
The 2026 World Cup is just around the corner, and for fans of the US Men's National Team (USMNT), the excitement is palpable. However, for those planning to follow their team throughout the tournament, the cost of doing so can be a significant factor. From tickets to hotels, and from food to merchandise, the expenses can add up quickly. In this article, we will delve into the various costs associated with following the USMNT during the 2026 World Cup.

## Ticket Prices
Ticket prices for the World Cup can vary greatly, depending on the stage of the tournament, the opponent, and the location of the match. According to the official FIFA website, ticket prices for the group stage matches range from $105 to $210. However, as the tournament progresses to the knockout stages, prices increase significantly, with tickets for the final match costing upwards of $1,100.

## Accommodation Costs
For fans planning to follow the USMNT throughout the tournament, accommodation costs can be a significant expense. Hotel prices in host cities tend to increase during the World Cup, with some hotels charging upwards of $500 per night. Alternative options, such as Airbnb or hostels, may be more budget-friendly, but prices can still range from $100 to $300 per night.

## Food and Beverage
Food and beverage costs can also add up quickly, especially for fans who plan to attend multiple matches. The cost of a meal at a mid-range restaurant can range from $15 to $30, while a beer at a stadium can cost upwards of $10. For fans who plan to purchase food and drinks at every match, these costs can add up to hundreds of dollars over the course of the tournament.

## Transportation
Transportation costs can also be a significant expense for fans who plan to follow the USMNT throughout the tournament. Flights, trains, and rental cars can be expensive, especially during peak travel seasons. According to Google Flights, a round-trip flight from the United States to a host city can cost upwards of $1,000.

## Merchandise
Finally, merchandise costs can also add up quickly, especially for fans who want to purchase jerseys, scarves, and other team gear. The cost of a USMNT jersey can range from $70 to $100, while a scarf can cost upwards of $20.

## Conclusion
Following the USMNT during the 2026 World Cup can be a costly endeavor, with expenses ranging from tickets to accommodations, food, transportation, and merchandise. However, for die-hard fans, the experience of cheering on their team in person can be priceless. By budgeting carefully and planning ahead, fans can minimize their expenses and make the most of their World Cup experience.

### Estimated Costs
* Tickets: $500 - $2,000
* Accommodation: $2,000 - $5,000
* Food and beverage: $500 - $1,000
* Transportation: $1,000 - $2,000
* Merchandise: $200 - $500
* Total: $4,200 - $10,500

By understanding the various costs associated with following the USMNT during the 2026 World Cup, fans can make informed decisions about their travel plans and budget accordingly. Whether you're a die-hard fan or just a casual observer, the World Cup is an event not to be missed, and with careful planning, you can make the most of your experience.
