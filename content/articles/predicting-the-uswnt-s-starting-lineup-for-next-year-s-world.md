# Introduction
The 2027 Women's World Cup is just around the corner, and the anticipation is building up. With Coach Emma Hayes at the helm, the USWNT is looking stronger than ever. As we approach the tournament, it's time to speculate about the starting lineup that Hayes will count on to bring home the coveted trophy.

## Current Roster and Trends
The current USWNT roster boasts an impressive array of talented players, each with their unique strengths and abilities. From seasoned veterans to young and ambitious newcomers, the team has a perfect blend of experience and fresh energy. Recent performances and trends suggest that Hayes will opt for a mix of defensive solidity and attacking flair in her starting lineup.

### Goalkeeper
The goalkeeper position is likely to be occupied by **Alyssa Naeher**, who has been a mainstay between the posts for the USWNT. Her exceptional shot-stopping abilities, combined with her impressive distribution skills, make her the top choice for Hayes.

### Defenders
The back four is expected to comprise of **Crystal Dunn** at left-back, **Tierna Davidson** and **Naomi Girma** as the central defenders, and **Kelley O'Hara** at right-back. This quartet offers a balance of defensive prowess, set-piece expertise, and attacking threat from the flanks.

### Midfielders
The midfield trio will probably consist of **Lindsey Horan**, **Rose Lavelle**, and **Samantha Mewis**. This combination provides a blend of creativity, work rate, and goal-scoring ability, allowing the USWNT to control the tempo of the game and create scoring opportunities.

### Forwards
The front three is likely to feature **Alex Morgan**, **Megan Rapinoe**, and **Trinity Rodman**. This attacking trio boasts a potent mix of speed, skill, and finishing ability, making them a nightmare for opposing defenses to deal with.

## Conclusion
While predicting the starting lineup is always subject to change due to injuries, form, and tactical preferences, the above-mentioned 11 players are likely to form the core of the USWNT's starting lineup for the 2027 Women's World Cup. With their exceptional talent, depth, and Hayes' tactical acumen, the USWNT is well-equipped to make a deep run in the tournament and bring home the championship.
