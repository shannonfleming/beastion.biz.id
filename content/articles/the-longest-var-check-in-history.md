## The VAR System: A Double-Edged Sword
The introduction of VAR (Video Assistant Referee) was intended to reduce controversy and improve accuracy in football. However, the Afcon final incident highlights the challenges of implementing this technology. On one hand, VAR has helped to correct numerous errors and ensure justice is served. On the other hand, its reliance on human interpretation and the potential for technical glitches can lead to delays and disputes.

### The Human Element
One of the primary concerns surrounding VAR is the human element. Referees, like any other individual, are prone to making mistakes. The Afcon final incident demonstrates how a referee's hesitation can exacerbate the situation, leading to prolonged delays and frustration. To mitigate this, referees must be trained to make swift and decisive decisions, rather than relying solely on the VAR system.
