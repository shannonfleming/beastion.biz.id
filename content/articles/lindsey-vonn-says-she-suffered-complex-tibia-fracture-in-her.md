**Lindsey Vonn, the renowned American alpine skier, has announced that she suffered a complex tibia fracture in her Olympic downhill crash.** 
## Context / Background
Lindsey Vonn is a highly decorated skier with numerous Olympic and World Championship medals to her name. Her recent crash during the Olympic downhill event has raised concerns about her career and future in the sport.

> Lindsey Vonn has stated that she has 'no regrets' about her decision to participate in the Olympic downhill event, despite the severe injury she sustained.

## What's Next?
Lindsey Vonn will require multiple surgeries to recover from her complex tibia fracture. Her father has suggested that this crash may mark the end of her illustrious career.
