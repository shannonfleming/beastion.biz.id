DR Congo will undoubtedly pose a challenge for England, with their physicality and defensive solidity making them a difficult team to break down. However, with Mainoo's clever passing and vision, England may be able to find ways to exploit the opposition's defense and create scoring opportunities.

## Rooney's Expertise

His suggestion to start Mainoo over Anderson is likely based on a thorough understanding of the team's strengths and weaknesses, as well as the opposition's tendencies.

The decision to start Kobbie Mainoo over Elliot Anderson is a bold one, but it may ultimately prove to be the right call.
