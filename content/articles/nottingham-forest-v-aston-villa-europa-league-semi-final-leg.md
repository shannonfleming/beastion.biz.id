# Introduction
The Europa League semi-final first leg match between Nottingham Forest and Aston Villa is set to be an exciting encounter. Both teams have shown impressive form in the competition so far, and this match will be crucial in determining the outcome of the tie.

## Team Strategies
Nottingham Forest are likely to employ a direct approach, using their pacey wingers to attack the Villa defense. On the other hand, Aston Villa will look to build through the middle, condensing the play and looking for quick interchanges. Ollie Watkins will be a key player for Villa, attacking the space in behind the Forest defense, especially in the absence of Murillo.

### Key Players
* Morgan Gibbs-White will be crucial for Forest, providing creativity and flair in midfield.
* Emi Buendia and John McGinn will look to feed Ollie Watkins with through balls, exploiting the space behind the Forest defense.
* Youri Tielemans will conduct the play from deep, arriving on the edge of the box to hit shots and create scoring opportunities.

## Tactical Analysis
The absence of Murillo will be a significant blow for Forest, as he provides defensive solidity and leadership. Villa will look to exploit this weakness, using their pace and movement to stretch the Forest defense. The midfield battle will be intense, with both teams looking to gain control and dictate the tempo of the game.

### Set Pieces
Set pieces will be an important aspect of the game, with both teams looking to capitalize on any mistakes made by the opposition. Forest will look to use their height and physicality to attack Villa's defense, while Villa will look to use their pace and movement to catch Forest off guard.

## Conclusion
The Europa League semi-final first leg match between Nottingham Forest and Aston Villa promises to be an exciting and closely contested encounter. With the return leg still to come, this match will be crucial in determining the outcome of the tie and which team will progress to the final.
